# FavPoll — For Charities

## A new kind of fundraising for life events

Memorial collections, birthday fundraisers, retirement donations — life events generate significant charitable giving every year. FavPoll makes that giving more personal, more engaging, and more likely to happen at all.

Instead of a collection tin or a generic donation page, FavPoll gives guests something to participate in. They pledge against their favourite things — favourite colour, favourite song, favourite film — and watch the rankings update in real time as others do the same. The donation becomes part of the occasion.

Some FavPolls carry a reveal — a secret about the person being honoured, shown to each guest after they pledge. It turns a transaction into a moment of connection. Guests remember it.

---

## How it works for your charity

**An organiser creates a FavPoll event** — a memorial, birthday, or other occasion — and selects your charity as the beneficiary. They choose poll topics, invite guests, and share a link.

**Guests pledge on their phones.** No app required. They select their favourites, choose an amount, and pay by card. The process takes under two minutes.

**Funds are disbursed to your charity automatically** when the poll closes, via Stripe. You receive a full breakdown of the event, total raised, and donor count. No manual processing required on your side.

**The event organiser manages everything.** You don't need to be involved in individual events — FavPoll handles the platform, the payments, and the donor experience.

## Planned giving

Some people include a FavPoll in their will — specifying your charity as the beneficiary, writing the questions and reveals in advance. When the time comes, an executor follows the instructions and creates the FavPoll. You receive the proceeds automatically when it closes.

This is a new form of planned giving that engages an entire community at the moment of loss, not just the individual donor.

## The live display

At gatherings — memorial services, birthday parties, retirement dinners — organisers can project a live FavPoll display on screen. Guests watch the rankings update in real time as pledges come in. It transforms a passive collection into a shared, visible experience that people remember.

This is a new kind of fundraising moment that no other platform offers.

## Fees and compliance

**Platform fee:** 5% of each donation, deducted before disbursement. The remaining 95% reaches your charity in full. Donors are shown the fee clearly before confirming their pledge — nothing is hidden.

**Payment processing:** handled by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment provider used by thousands of charities worldwide. Card data never touches FavPoll's servers.

**Stripe Connect:** FavPoll uses Stripe Connect to hold and disburse funds on behalf of charities. Your charity receives funds directly into your designated account. FavPoll does not hold charitable funds beyond the duration of a poll (maximum 90 days, typically much less).

**Fundraising permission:** FavPoll requires written confirmation from your charity before listing you as a beneficiary. This ensures compliance with the Charities Act and gives you full visibility and control over how your name is used.

**Gift Aid:** FavPoll does not currently process Gift Aid declarations. Donors wishing to claim Gift Aid should be directed to your charity's own Gift Aid process. We are exploring a direct integration for future releases.
